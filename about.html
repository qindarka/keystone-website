Keystone Technologies has spent over a decade helping Southwestern Ontario businesses turn IT from a fire-fighting cost centre into a real strategic asset.

Our story

An MSP that picks up the phone

Keystone was founded on a simple idea: small and medium-sized businesses deserve the same caliber of IT support as enterprises — without the bureaucracy or the bill.

We're a Southwestern Ontario company with technicians who know your environment, an account manager who knows your name, and engineers on call when things matter most. We don't outsource your business to a national queue.

Today we support municipalities, property managers, professional service firms and growing businesses across the region — managing networks, defending against threats, and modernizing the technology that runs their operations.
