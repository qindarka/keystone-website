Best Mobile Apps to Help Grow your Small Business

May 29, 2018 · John Woods

Trying to get your small business off the ground is difficult with limited resources. However, true entrepreneurs know how to make limited resources go a long way to overcome obstacles. Luckily, some of the best way to grow your business can be found at your fingertips. Yes, we’re talking about mobile apps for your phone.

However, with so many apps out there, where do you start? We’ve done the research for your and found the top mobile apps that businesses are using today.

Square

What it is:

Square is point of sale software that runs off your iOS device. It allows you to accept debit and credit cards, keep track of sales and send receipts. The best part? Money gets deposited in your account within 1 or 2 business days.

How businesses are using it:

The advantage of Square running off your device is that you can use it wherever you are. For businesses without permanent store fronts, it’s useful for popup events, markets and festivals. However, it’s becoming more common to find businesses using square in storefronts and retail environments due to its affordable fees and user-friendly features.

Udemy

What it is:

Udemy offers thousands of online courses. You can learn anything from new languages to how to use software to leadership skills.

How businesses are using it:

Small business owners wear many hats. They have to manage several areas they have no formal training in like accounting, sales, and HR. Udemy teaches you valuable skills, allowing you to be confident in managing all areas of your business and saving you from having to pay someone to take over these tasks. At least long enough until you grow enough to hire more staff.

Don’t have the money to offer your employees competitive training or tuition reimbursement? Allow them to use Udemy on company time instead.

Shopify

What it is:

Shopify offers ecommerce software for retail businesses. Shopify ecommerce solutions are great for both online and offline retailers and it allows businesses to customize their online store, manage and fulfill orders and view detailed analytics about sales and growth.

How businesses are using it:

Shopify offers a variety of packages and pricing levels so it’s a great option for entrepreneurs, start ups and small businesses. However, it’s sophisticated enough that some of their biggest customers include Tesla motors and Penguin books. Shopify’s mobile app is perfect for on-the-go business owners, allowing them to manage their store from their device.

Logo Maker

What it is:

Logo Maker allows you to easily create a professional looking logo right from your mobile device. It includes hundreds of templates and fonts to create a unique and personalized logo that showcases your brand.

How businesses are using it:

Rather than paying for expensive graphic design software, or outsourcing graphic design services, Logo Maker helps you create a professional brand at a price you can afford. As a start-up, it’s a great app to help cut costs while building your brand’s value.
